Minetest mod: repel
Version: 1.0
Author: cmdskp
===================
Depends:
--------
default

Description:
------------
A Minetest 0.4.16 to 5.5.0 mod to repel players trying to dig through protected area blocks.
It teleports them back to where they starting digging the protected node and zeros their acceleration.

Can work with any protection mods, providing they are loaded first in the depends.txt.
(Currently, both Protector and Areas mods are listed in the depends.txt to ensure they load first.)
